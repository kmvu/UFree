About UFree

UFree is a social scheduling app that helps you coordinate with friends by sharing your weekly availability. Your schedule is only visible to your Trusted Circleâ€”friends you've mutually connected with.

Features

  • 7-Day Availability Tracking: Mark your schedule as Busy, Free, Morning Only, Afternoon Only, or Evening Only.
  • Trusted Circle: Share your availability only with friends you trust.
  • Privacy-First Design: Contact matching uses hashed phone numbers. Raw contacts are never stored on our servers.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. We use cryptographic hashing for contact discovery and Firebase security to protect your data. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
