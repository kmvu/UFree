Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

UFree ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle user data, specifically regarding our Trusted Circle friend-matching feature.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account Info: Your display name and phone number (used for authentication and discovery).
  • Schedule Data: Your 7-day availability status (Busy, Free, Morning Only, Afternoon Only, Evening Only).

2. Contact List Privacy (Important)

To help you find your trusted friends, UFree can sync with your device's contact list. Here is how we protect that data:

  • Hashing: Before your contacts leave your device, we convert phone numbers into cryptographic hashes (SHA-256). We do not upload raw phone numbers of your contacts.
  • Matching Only: We only use these hashes to check if any of your contacts are already registered UFree users.
  • No Storage of Non-Users: If a contact is not a UFree user, their hash is immediately discarded and never stored on our servers.
  • No Names Collected: We do not collect or store the names, addresses, or emails of people in your contact list.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties. Your schedule is only visible to users whom you have mutually connected with (your "Trusted Circle").

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We store your schedule data only as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

5. Security

We use industry-standard Firebase security protocols and Firestore Security Rules to ensure that only authorized friends can access your availability data.
